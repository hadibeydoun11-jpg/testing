How to play

Race your pawn to the opposite row.

Each turn: move your pawn OR place a wall.

Walls block paths but cannot completely trap a player.

Each player has 10 walls to use.
Controls

M — Move mode
W — Wall mode
H / V — Wall direction
N — New game
